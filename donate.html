Other Ways to Give

By Mail: Please make checks payable to the Manhattan Institute and mail to: Manhattan Institute for Policy Research, 52 Vanderbilt Avenue, New York, NY 10017.

Planned Giving: Include the Manhattan Institute in your estate plans through a bequest, charitable remainder trust, or other planned giving vehicle. Contact our development team for more information.

Corporate & Foundation Giving: The Manhattan Institute welcomes support from corporations, foundations, and donor-advised funds. Please contact us to discuss how your organization can partner with MI.

Securities: Donating appreciated securities can provide significant tax advantages. Please contact our development office at (212) 599-7000 or email info@manhattan-institute.org for transfer instructions.
